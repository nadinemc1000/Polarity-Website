Adapting to Social Situations.

The growing disconnect between the participatory nature of contemporary entertainment and passive design of our furniture urges a dynamic system for support.

With Polarity, we envision a new category of seating. One that is modular, conversation-first, and blends sculptural mid-century inspiration with modern adaptability. Our system allows users to build social settings or comfortable spaces that also grow with their lives.

This is not just a couch. It's a platform for connection, adaptability, and long-term living.

Adaptable Backrest

A seating system that responds to how people interact. The adjustable backrest allows users to:

  • Shift between open, social configurations and private lounging
  • Support different postures, from upright conversation to relaxed reclining
  • Reorient seating without needing to rearrange the entire system

Modular Design

Built to evolve with your life. Each unit functions as part of a flexible system:

  • Add or remove components as your space or needs change
  • Reconfigure layouts for hosting, relaxing, or everyday living
  • Adapt across different situations and spaces

Third Function

More than seating, a spatial system. Our design introduces a third function:

  • Acts as a social anchor for gatherings
  • Creates zones within open spaces
  • Encourages movement, interaction, and shared experiences
